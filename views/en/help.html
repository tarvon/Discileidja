Frequently asked questions

I found a disc - what should I do?
If you have found someone's disc, you should go to the Found a disc page and insert all the features of the disc. Then the owner of the disc can find it and contact you directly!

How can I look for my lost disc?
If you have lost a disc, go to our Lost a disc page. Search for our disc there and you'll be able to contact the finder after you identify yourself.
